BridgeLabz is an AWS recognized Incubator focused on solving the tech employability challenge. It aims to solve the problem of low employability of engineers in India, by expanding the employability pool with its ‘Maker Program’. The “Maker Program” is an unique experiential setup where fresh engineers learn coding by making cutting edge products themselves from scratch.

Propelled by an outcome-focused approach, the Maker Program is a 4-month long fellowship program.
